## 1. Foundation

### What is your website about?

My website is about marine life and its importance to the world. It includes information about animals, ecosystems, and environmental issues. The goal is to educate people and raise awareness about the oceans.

### Who is this website for?

The website is for anyone interested in learning about marine life. It is especially aimed at beginners who want simple and clear information about the ocean.

### Objectives

1. Help people learn about ocean ecosystems  
2. Raise awareness about environmental problems  
3. Show how people can help protect the ocean  
4. Share facts and interesting information

## 4. Design Principles

### Contrast

Headings are larger and darker than text. Buttons use accent colors.

### Repetition

Same layout, colors, and navigation across all pages.

### Alignment

Content centered with max width.

### Proximity

Related content grouped into sections.
